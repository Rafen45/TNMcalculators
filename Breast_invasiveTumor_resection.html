Histologic Type

Determination of histologic type is based on routine histologic examination; special stains such as e-cadherin are not required for determining histologic type. The histologic type corresponds to the largest carcinoma. If there are smaller carcinomas of a different type, this information should be included under “Additional Pathologic Findings.”
NB! Special type carcinomas should consist of at least 90% pure pattern.
